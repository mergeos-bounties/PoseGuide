# Difficulty Levels Guide for Pose Packs

## Overview

This guide defines the difficulty levels for pose packs in PoseGuide, helping users choose appropriate poses for their skill level.

## Difficulty Levels

### Beginner (⭐)
**Target Audience**: New users, casual creators
**Time to Complete**: 5-15 minutes
**Complexity**: Simple, straightforward poses

#### Characteristics
- Minimal body positioning required
- Common, natural poses
- Limited technical knowledge needed
- Basic camera angles

#### Example Poses
- Standing neutral
- Sitting on chair
- Walking forward
- Simple hand gestures

#### Tips for Beginners
- Start with basic standing poses
- Focus on natural body alignment
- Use reference images for guidance
- Don't rush - take your time

---

### Intermediate (⭐⭐)
**Target Audience**: Regular users, hobbyists
**Time to Complete**: 15-30 minutes
**Complexity**: Moderate complexity

#### Characteristics
- Multiple body parts positioning
- Dynamic poses with movement
- Some technical knowledge helpful
- Varied camera angles

#### Example Poses
- Dance moves
- Action poses (running, jumping)
- Social interactions (handshake, hug)
- Professional poses (presentation, demo)

#### Tips for Intermediate
- Practice body proportion consistency
- Pay attention to weight distribution
- Use reference videos for dynamic poses
- Experiment with different angles

---

### Advanced (⭐⭐⭐)
**Target Audience**: Experienced users, professionals
**Time to Complete**: 30-60 minutes
**Complexity**: Complex, detailed poses

#### Characteristics
- intricate body positioning
- Complex interactions with objects
- Technical knowledge required
- Professional camera work

#### Example Poses
- Complex dance choreography
- Athletic activities (gymnastics, martial arts)
- Group interactions (multiple characters)
- Dramatic/emotional expressions

#### Tips for Advanced
- Study anatomy and body mechanics
- Use motion capture references
- Pay attention to finger positioning
- Practice complex weight shifts

---

### Expert (⭐⭐⭐⭐)
**Target Audience**: Professionals, artists
**Time to Complete**: 60+ minutes
**Complexity**: Highly complex, artistic

#### Characteristics
- Artistic, expressive poses
- Complex object interactions
- Deep technical expertise needed
- Cinematic camera work

#### Example Poses
- Artistic dance performances
- Martial arts sequences
- Complex emotional scenes
- Character-driven narratives

#### Tips for Expert
- Study professional references
- Understand body language psychology
- Master weight and balance
- Create compelling narratives

---

### Master (⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐)
**Target Audience**: Masters, educators
**Time to Complete**: Variable
**Complexity**: Artistic mastery

#### Characteristics
- Unique, signature poses
- Innovative body language
- Teaching/educational focus
- Pushing creative boundaries

#### Example Poses
- Signature style poses
- Educational demonstrations
- Experimental art poses
- Cultural/traditional poses

#### Tips for Master
- Develop your unique style
- Teach others your techniques
- Push creative boundaries
- Preserve cultural poses

---

## Usage Guidelines

### For Users
1. **Start Simple**: Begin with beginner poses
2. **Progress Gradually**: Move to intermediate as you improve
3. **Practice Regularly**: Consistent practice improves skills
4. **Use References**: Always use reference images/videos

### For Creators
1. **Label Clearly**: Mark difficulty on all poses
2. **Provide Instructions**: Include step-by-step guidance
3. **Offer Variations**: Provide easier/harder variations
4. **Include References**: Add reference images
